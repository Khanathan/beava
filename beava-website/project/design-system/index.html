01

Foundations

the vibe

Three warm hues on a cream page. Serif for headlines, sans for everything else. Generous whitespace. Borders do the work; shadows are subtle. One hit of orange per viewport. No emoji in product UI. Cream beats white every time.

Cream, not white

Page background is always #fdfaf4. Pure white feels clinical and breaks the warmth.

Orange, sparingly

Burnt orange is for one or two things per viewport — the primary CTA, the active nav, a live dot.

Borders over shadows

We lean on a 1px warm rule (#e6dccb) for card edges. Shadows are brown-tinted and low.

Shadows

warm & low

Four elevation tiers, all brown-tinted. Never blue or cool gray. Never colored glows (no orange halos). The --shadow-pop chunky sticker shadow is reserved for playful moments (capability tiles, blog chapter cards).

06

Motion

ease-out by default

Default entrance: fade + translateY(8px), 200ms ease-out. No parallax, no scroll-linked zooms. Spring easing is reserved for mascot moments only — never on generic UI. Reduce-motion is honored globally.

08

Mascot & logo

hand-drawn, no stock

The beaver is our mascot, not a brand personality we roleplay. Studio-Ghibli-adjacent, rounded, thick outlines, flat fills with minimal shading. Never stock photography, 3D renders, gradient orbs, or glassmorphism.

09

Voice & tone

warm, not cute

We sit in the triangle between Julia Evans (warm, pedagogical), Patrick McKenzie (precise, numerate), and DuckDB (indie-OSS, mascot-forward, a bit cheeky). Everything should feel like it was written by a specific human who ships. Sentence case everywhere. Oxford commas. Em-dashes are house style.

✓ Yes

"Real-time features without the streaming priesthood. beava is a single-binary feature server. HTTP in, HTTP out. No Kafka to babysit."

× No

"🚀 Excited to announce the GA of our enterprise-ready AI-powered stream processing platform! 🎉"

10

Don'ts

keep the vibe

No pure white

Page bg is always cream. White at page level breaks the warmth.

No emoji in product UI

Reserve 🦫 for blog/Discord. UI uses the real mascot art or typographic arrows (→).

No stock photos

Ever. Illustrations only, hand-drawn, matching the mascot style.

No Title Case

Sentence case for headings, buttons, nav. Always.

No cold shadows

All shadows are brown-tinted rgba(26,23,20,x). Never gray-blue.

No gradient orbs

Backgrounds are flat cream. 2% paper grain is the only allowed texture.
