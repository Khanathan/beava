Create LOGO_PROMPT.md at the project root containing:

1. A brief context section explaining the product (2-3 sentences so the reader understands what they are prompting for).

2. Three distinct prompt variants, each targeting a different mascot/logo concept:

**Variant A — "The Tally Counter Creature"**
A goofy cartoon creature (think blob-like or round animal) that IS a living tally counter / abacus. It has big expressive eyes, a wide grin, and its body incorporates visual elements of counting (tally marks, abacus beads, counter digits). Streams of colorful data events flow into one side of the creature and neat, organized feature cards pop out the other side. The creature looks fast — motion lines, speed streaks. Style: flat vector mascot logo, bold outlines, limited color palette (2-3 colors + black), suitable for favicon and README badge. Background: transparent/white. Think ScyllaDB monster meets a retro mechanical counter.

**Variant B — "The Stream Goblin"**
A mischievous, smiling goblin/imp character sitting on top of a single glowing binary/cube (representing "one binary"). The goblin is catching streams of sparkly data particles with one hand and tossing out polished gem-like "features" with the other. The goblin wears a tiny hardhat or visor (ops-free = no work needed). Cartoon style, goofy proportions (big head, small body), bold flat colors. Style: mascot logo suitable for dark and light backgrounds, vector art, tech startup aesthetic but playful.

**Variant C — "The Speed Tally Bird"**
A cartoonish bird (like a roadrunner or swift) made of flowing stream lines, mid-sprint. Its tail feathers are data streams, its beak holds a tiny clipboard with tally marks. The bird has comically oversized sneakers or rocket boots (speed). One wing is raised showing "events in", the other showing "features out" in a thought-bubble style. Flat vector illustration, mascot style, bold black outlines, 2-3 accent colors. Personality: fast, fun, slightly chaotic but reliable.

3. A "Modifier Stacks" section with add-on phrases the user can mix in:
   - For more goofiness: "exaggerated cartoon proportions, squash and stretch, googly eyes"
   - For more professionalism: "clean vector logo, suitable for business card, minimal detail"
   - For icon/favicon: "simple silhouette version, recognizable at 16x16px, monochrome variant"
   - For dark mode: "works on dark background, bright accent colors, subtle glow effects"
   - Style references: "in the style of tech company mascots like ScyllaDB, DigitalOcean Sammy, Docker Moby, Go Gopher"

4. A "Negative Prompt" section (things to exclude): "no photorealism, no 3D rendering, no gradients, no text in the image, no generic clip art, no corporate stiffness, no Kafka/streaming infrastructure imagery"

Each prompt should be a single copy-pasteable paragraph (no line breaks within the prompt itself) so it works directly in any image generation tool.
