## Copyright attribution

| State | Value | Compliance |
|-------|-------|------------|
| Pre-plan | `Copyright 2025-2026 Tally Contributors` | Wrong per D-04 (`hoang@beava.dev`); uses internal repo codename `tally` which Workstream-D will scrub from history |
| Post-plan | `Copyright 2026 Hoang Phan` | Matches D-04 author convention (`Hoang Phan <hoang@beava.dev>`); year is project's first public-release year (v0.0.0 ships 2026) |

**Convention chosen:** `Copyright 2026 Hoang Phan` (single-name attribution, no "Beava contributors" / "Hoang Phan and contributors" qualifier). Rationale:

- Phase 13.8 ships v0.0.0; project has had no external contributors yet at GA, so `and Beava contributors` would be aspirational rather than accurate.
- D-04 mailmap rewrites every commit author/committer to `Hoang Phan <hoang@beava.dev>`; the LICENSE attribution mirrors that single-author state.
- Future contributors gain copyright on their own contributions automatically under Apache-2.0 §5 (Submission of Contributions); they do not need to be enumerated in the LICENSE file.
- A future plan can amend this to `Copyright 2026-2027 Hoang Phan and Beava contributors` once external PRs land — cheap edit, no compliance impact.
