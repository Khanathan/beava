<objective>
Clean up stale `worktree-agent-*` branches accumulated under `.claude/worktrees/` and the underlying `.git/refs/heads/worktree-agent-*` refs (and any `.claude/worktrees/<name>/` directory tree where the parent worktree no longer exists). These branches are local-only artifacts of past parallel agent runs; they don't ship to the public repo (history-rewrite Plan 11 strips them anyway), but cleaning them BEFORE the rewrite reduces ref-graph clutter and prevents accidental re-push to the public remote.

Per CONTEXT.md D-08 Workstream-D-12 ("Strip ~30+ stale worktree-agent-* branches in `.claude/worktrees/`; `git worktree prune`").

NOTE on wave assignment: This plan is in Wave 1 (parallel with Workstream-C) because worktree-cleanup is a pure local-tree operation that does NOT touch published commit content; it does not depend on the filter-repo rehearsal output, and filter-repo will strip these branches anyway in Plan 11. Running it earlier just makes the dev tree tidier.

Purpose: Workstream-D requirement; reduces ref-graph noise.

Output: Cleanup report; refs deleted; worktree pruned.
</objective>
