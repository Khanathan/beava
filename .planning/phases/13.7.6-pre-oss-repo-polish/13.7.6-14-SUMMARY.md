## Decisions Made

- **`Copyright 2026 Hoang Phan` (single-author form).** Previous attribution `Copyright 2025-2026 Tally Contributors` used the internal codename `tally` (which Workstream-D scrubs from history) and an aspirational `Contributors` qualifier. D-04 locks every commit author/committer to `Hoang Phan <hoang@beava.dev>`; the LICENSE attribution mirrors that single-author state. External contributors gain copyright on their own contributions automatically under Apache-2.0 §5.
- **Year = 2026 (single year, not range).** v0.0.0 ships in 2026; pre-2026 commits were internal-codename-era and are not yet a publicly-released work. A future plan can amend to `2026-2027` once the second calendar year of public commits exists — cheap edit.
- **Replace whole LICENSE rather than patch the 4 body deviations.** The pre-existing 4 body deviations (3 word-level + 4-line APPENDIX truncation) are individually harmless but collectively make `licensee` / SPDX-tooling flag the file as `Apache-2.0 (modified)`. A wholesale replacement reduces the diff to a single expected line (the copyright fill-in) and gives SPDX detectors a clean `Apache-2.0` match.
