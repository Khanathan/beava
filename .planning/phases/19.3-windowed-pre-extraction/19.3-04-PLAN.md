<objective>
Land sub-goal **19.3-C**: hoist `ExtractedFields` build above the per-descriptor loop in `apply_event_to_aggregations`. Currently rebuilt per-desc at `agg_apply.rs:201-205` (5× per event for fraud-team's 5 descs); after this plan, built ONCE per event from the per-event field union stored on `EventDescriptor.apply_field_names`.

Per RESEARCH.md amendment, this work expands to **4 sub-tasks** (a/b/c/d) because the apparently-existing infrastructure has gaps:
- `EventDescriptor.apply_field_names` is declared at `registry.rs:74` but currently `vec![]` at all 15+ construction sites — registration-time population pass is missing.
- `feat.descriptor.field_idx` currently indexes into the per-agg `agg.field_names`; must shift to index into the per-event `apply_field_names` union — cross-cutting re-resolution.
- `ExtractedFields` SmallVec inline cap of 8 (`agg_op.rs:232`) spills to heap on fraud-team Txn's ~12-field per-event union; must widen to 16.

Predicted lift: ~600 ns/event saved (per `19.2-INVESTIGATION.md` §4 19.3-C row). Stacks on Plans 19.3-02 + 19.3-03; agg-stage drops from ~9,050 → ~8,450 ns; EPS ~107k → ~115-125k.

Purpose: eliminate the per-descriptor row.get fan-out tax (Plan 19.2-01 was per-agg; this is the per-event generalization).
Output: 4 atomic red→green commit pairs (one per sub-task) + measurable zero per-event ExtractedFields allocations on warm path.
</objective>
