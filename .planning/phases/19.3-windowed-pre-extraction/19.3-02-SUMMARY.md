## Root cause

Cost model in `19.2-INVESTIGATION.md §4` was conjectured from `per_call_ns × call_count_per_event` arithmetic, never validated against a flamegraph. Three structural errors compounded (full analysis in `19.3-COST-MODEL.md`):
1. **43/88 windowed features**, not 60/88 (verified by parsing fraud-team.json directly).
2. **Per-call savings ~30 ns**, not 65 ns. Investigation conjectured `~50 ns row.get + 5 ns where re-eval + 10 ns dispatch`; actual workload showed the elimination saves much less because the inner state's `update_pre` already short-circuited row.get for many Tier-1 kinds.
3. **`update_at`'s own overhead** (`Row::new()` per call + `pre_val` indexed extract + 9-arg recursive dispatch) added back ~30-50 ns/call.
