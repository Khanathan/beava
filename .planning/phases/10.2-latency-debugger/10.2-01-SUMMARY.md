## Components
- **Histogram**: 30 log-spaced bins (1us-10ms), record() via binary search, percentile() via linear interpolation
- **RollingHistogram**: two-buffer swap every 2.5 min, idle decay after 5 min
- **SlowQueryHeap**: BinaryHeap<Reverse<T>> bounded at 20, O(log 20) insert
- **LatencyTracker**: 4 command histograms + per-stream PUSH map + 4 slow-query heaps
- **CommandKind**: enum (Push=0, Get=1, Set=2, Mset=3)
