## Sanity Assessment

All 28 numbers are within expected orders of magnitude for an Apple M4 chip:
- Hot aggregation ops (count, sum, avg) are 1–10 ns per call — headroom for ≥3M EPS/core
- `apply/3agg_100ent_1Kevt` at 1.01 ms for 1K events = ~1 µs/event end-to-end — consistent with target
- `eval/arith` at 110 ns is marginally above the informal "< 100 ns" guidance — noted, no action needed in this measurement-only phase
- Python SDK compile at 110 µs for a 10-descriptor DAG — well under the 10 ms threshold
