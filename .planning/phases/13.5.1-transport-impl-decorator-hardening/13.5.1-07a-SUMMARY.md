- **Commits:** `cead2a08` (buffer slice), `dd44c3b0` (geo slice piggybacked), `db737725` (recency slice piggybacked)

**Justification:** Pre-existing tests encoded the *wrong* contract; the new contract is the canonical one per `docs/operators/` and the v0 acceptance suite. Rule 1 (bugs DIRECTLY caused by the current task's changes) — without this inline fix, the signature reconciliation would have left the suite RED. No new test logic added; only call-site rewrites.
