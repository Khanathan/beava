### `python/beava/_table.py` (NEW, 143 LOC)
- `TableDescriptor` with `_name / _key_cols / _chain / _parent / _kind="table"`
- `table(...)` decorator with three call shapes:
  - `@bv.table(key="user_id")` — keyed (single column)
  - `@bv.table(key=["a", "b"])` — keyed (composite)
  - `@bv.table` (no parens) and `@bv.table()` (parens, no kwarg) — global per ADR-003 Decision B
- `_resolve_upstream_proxies` mirrors the events PEP-563 fix
- Function form only (class form deferred to v0.1+)
