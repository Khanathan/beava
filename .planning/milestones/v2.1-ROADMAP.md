## Key decisions

- **Admin access** via SSH tunnel + loopback — no bearer-token endpoint on public Caddy (Caddy strips Authorization). Zero attack-surface at launch.
- **Rate-limit** stanza commented out — `caddy-ratelimit` not in stock apt; launch blast radius small; documented in `deploy/README.md`.
- **Defense in depth** — `--tcp-bind 127.0.0.1` + `ufw deny 6400/6401` + Caddy edge-404 of admin paths.
- **Smoke invariant 6** (TCP 6400 unreachable) uses `nc` or bash `/dev/tcp` fallback; assertion negated.
- **v0 port** — all artifacts ported to v0 SDK under Phase 26-03; no re-provision needed on VM.

## Why live-run is async

Phase 20 engineering is done. The 5-day live observation is purely calendar-bound (needs 5 real days of wall clock with crash-recovery verified mid-run). It runs async behind the launch window — nothing engineering-side blocks on it, and it does not block v0 Local Replica (Phases 27-34). Milestone officially closes upon successful LIVE_SIGNOFF.md completion; this archive is the engineering-close record.
